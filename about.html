About

Hello, my name is Dave Glick.

I am currently the Principal Software Engineer at the Securities Investor Protection Corporation (SIPC) where I lead the design and development of line-of-business web applications using .NET technologies like ASP.NET MVC, Entity Framework, and client libraries like jQuery, KendoUI, and Bootstrap.

Of course, we're all hopefully much more than where we plant our butt during the week (or stand, if you're one of those standing desk folks). At home I am a husband and father to three very active kids and enjoy every moment. That doesn't leave a lot of left-over time, but when I do get the chance I enjoy taking pictures and a good book. Somehow, I also occasionally find time to write code.
